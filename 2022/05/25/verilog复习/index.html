convert 2.384 to 8 bits with 6 fractional bits:

  1. 2.384 × 26 = 152.576
  2. Round to 153
  3. Convert to 1001 1001
  4. Result is 10.011001 = 2.390625 (=153/64)
  5. Error is 0.006625 (absolute), or 0.28% (relative)

  1. –6.73 × 25 = –215.36
  2. Round to –215
  3. Convert to 1100101001
